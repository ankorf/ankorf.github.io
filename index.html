Doctoral Research

Demand Forecasting Techniques in the Transportation and Hospitality Sector Using New Technologies
Institution: Ionian University, Department of Tourism  •  Status: In progress (admitted 2024)  •  Language: English
Focus: Services Marketing • Data Analytics • Operations Research
Keywords: Demand Forecasting • Machine Learning • Tourism • Hospitality • Transportation • Big Data • LSTM • ARIMA

The dissertation develops and evaluates advanced demand forecasting frameworks combining traditional statistical methods (ARIMA, exponential smoothing) with machine learning algorithms (LSTM, SVR, Random Forests). The research addresses forecasting under uncertainty and exogenous shocks, integrates real-time and big data sources, and produces scalable frameworks applicable to both large enterprises and small tourism businesses. Special emphasis on seasonal modelling, IoT data streams, and comparative evaluation (MAE, RMSE, MAPE) across methodologies.
